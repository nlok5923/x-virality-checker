# Privacy Policy for X Virality Checker

**Last Updated:** January 21, 2026

## Overview

X Virality Checker is a Chrome extension that analyzes your tweets using AI to provide virality insights and suggestions. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Information We Collect

When you use the extension to analyze a tweet, we collect:

1. **Tweet Content** - The text of the tweet you choose to analyze
2. **Follower Count** - Your X/Twitter follower count (if available on the page)
3. **User Bio** - Your X/Twitter bio (if available on the page)
4. **Analysis History** - Previous analyses (optional, stored locally only)
5. **User Preferences** - Your settings choices (stored locally only)

## How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

1. **Provide Analysis** - Send your tweet content to our backend API for AI-powered virality analysis
2. **Improve Accuracy** - Use your follower count and bio to provide more personalized predictions
3. **Save History** - Store your past analyses locally in your browser (if you enable this feature)
4. **Remember Preferences** - Save your settings locally for a better user experience

## Data Storage

### Local Storage (On Your Device)
- Analysis history (optional)
- User preferences and settings
- Usage statistics (analysis count and estimated costs)

### Our Servers
- **We DO NOT store your tweets** on our servers
- Tweet content is processed in real-time only
- No persistent storage of your data
- Requests are processed and immediately discarded

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

**Grok AI (by xAI)**
- Purpose: AI-powered tweet analysis
- Data Shared: Tweet content, follower count, user bio
- Privacy Policy: https://x.ai/legal/privacy-policy

Your tweet content is sent to Grok AI's API for analysis. Please review their privacy policy for information on how they handle data.

## Data Sharing

We **DO NOT**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes
- Track your browsing activity outside of X/Twitter
- Collect personally identifiable information

We **ONLY** share your tweet content with Grok AI for the purpose of analysis.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights:

1. **Clear History** - Delete all saved analyses at any time via extension settings
2. **Disable History** - Turn off history saving in the extension settings
3. **Control Analysis** - Choose which tweets to analyze (opt-in per tweet)
4. **Uninstall** - Remove the extension to delete all local data immediately

## Data Retention

- **Analysis History:** Stored locally until you clear it or uninstall the extension (max 50 entries)
- **Server Processing:** Tweet data is not retained after processing
- **Settings:** Stored locally until you change them or uninstall the extension

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

- HTTPS encryption for all API communications
- API keys stored securely on our server (never in the extension)
- No persistent server-side storage of your tweets
- Local data stored using Chrome's secure storage APIs

## Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
